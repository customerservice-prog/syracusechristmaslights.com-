Central New York's Trusted Holiday Lighting Crew

We're a local, established outdoor services business bringing the same reliability and attention to detail we're known for in the warmer months to holiday lighting. Our crews are fully insured, trained on safe ladder and roofline work, and equipped with commercial-grade LED lighting built to handle a full Syracuse winter. From a single-story ranch to a large multi-story home, we design a layout that fits your roofline, then handle setup, mid-season checks, and a tidy takedown once the holidays wrap up.

Services

  • Roofline and gutter lights - custom fit to your home's exact roofline, clipped in securely for wind and snow.
  • Tree and shrub wrapping - even wraps on ornamental trees, shrubs, and small evergreens.
  • Wreaths, garland, and accent lighting - lit garland, wreaths, and doorway accents for a finished look.
  • Walkway and pathway lighting - lit pathways, driveways, and walkways for a full-property glow.
  • Permanent, year-round light housing - a discreet track installed once, restring or change colors for any holiday or season.
  • Full takedown and seasonal storage - we remove everything after the season and can store lights until next year.
  • Commercial and storefront holiday lighting - storefronts, property entrances, and commercial buildings.
