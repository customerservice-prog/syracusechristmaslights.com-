# Market Research: Christmas Light Installation Pricing (Syracuse, NY area)

Compiled mid-2026 from aggregated third-party home-services cost guides (Angi, HomeAdvisor, Contractor+, Thumbtack, Eufy, renovetted.com) and local business listings. Treat as directional; re-verify periodically.

## Regional pricing benchmarks
- Per linear foot locally: roughly $3 to $15/ft, average near $7/ft.
- Labor-only (customer supplies lights): roughly $1.25 to $3/ft.
- Installer supplies lights: roughly $5 to $16/ft.

## Typical project totals by home size
- Small home or yard: $250 to $600
- Medium home, larger roofline or multiple trees: $600 to $1,500
- Large or complex, extensive roofline or custom design: $1,500 to $3,000
- Full-scale or commercial: $5,000 and up

## Common add-ons in the market
- Tree wrapping, priced per tree by size
- Takedown and removal, sometimes bundled, sometimes separate
- Storage between seasons
- Mid-season repairs and bulb replacement

## Local competitors identified
- Holiday Lighting of Rochester
- Christmas Decor by Michael Grimm Landscape and Tree Service, Jamesville NY
- CNY Roof Cleaners, offers light hanging as an add-on service
- Outdoor Lighting Perspectives of Central NY
- Majestic Holiday Lighting

## Strategic takeaways
- Price inside the local range, competitive but not the cheapest, to support a quality positioning.
- Offer flat tiered packages built from an underlying per-foot formula, since customers respond better to flat quotes than raw per-foot math.
- Bundle or clearly itemize removal so it does not feel like a surprise add-on.
- Biggest differentiator versus local competitors: an instant online estimate plus self-serve booking and deposit, rather than the lead-gen contact forms most competitors use.
